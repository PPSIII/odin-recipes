MilkSteak

Brief Summary of The Dish

This dish is one of the most exquisite foods to ever grace the buds of us peasant humans. I recommend eating this on a full stomach, preferably after enjoying a plate full of blanched rats eggs. Please cook extra well done.

Ingredients

  • 1 Steaklike meat(preferably shaved)
  • 5000 Uncooked Jelly Belly Beans
  • 1 Gallon of Whole Fat Milk(this is thick and jelly like, only can be obtained from a male cow's utters)
  • Water, to cool the steak in case it gets too hot
  • A plate, made of porcelain

Steps

  1. Put steak on radiator during the winter, it should be nice and hot. Should take about 55 minutes.
  2. Pour milk into spare shoe, tie show to ceiling fan at high speed. You will want to let it spin until the milk is nice and browned. Should take 3 mintues and 22 seconds.
  3. At minute 20, make sure to pour some water on that nice and hot steak. repeat this every 3 minutes moving forward.
  4. 50 minutes have passed, that steak should be nice and ready now. Put it on a plate and pour the milk on top, every last drop.
  5. Finish the plate with a nice garnish of jelly beans, you should only have 30 left after snacking on the other 5000 for the past 54 minutes.
  6. Enjoy :)
