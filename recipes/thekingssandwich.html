The King's Sandwich

Brief Summary of The Dish

This dish is best eaten after a long day on the job. Nothing better to wash down the day at the office than this baby.

Ingredients

  • Fried Chicken, preferably from the Master Commander of Chicken Himself
  • 1 lb of American Cheese
  • 3 gallons of corn oil
  • (optional) A cauldron or a deep fryer of some sort

Steps

  1. Dip fried chicken into the deep fryer for 5 minutes total
  2. Dip pound of cheese into the deep fryer for 20 minutes
  3. Combine fried chicken and cheese into a sandwich like configuration
  4. Dip sandwich into deep fryer for 10 minutes
  5. Enjoy
