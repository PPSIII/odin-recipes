Chicken & Beans

A Brief Summary of The Dish

This dish consists of one of the rarest ingredients known to man: Stage Fright

Ingredients

  • Chicken, no feathers
  • Beans, grown naturally from a can
  • Lyrics, something from your heart

Steps

  1. Place the chicken and beans in a plastic shopping bag together
  2. Call your local bars to see who is having an open mic night and book it
  3. Bring the bag in on performance night, make sure to dump contents onto a stool in front of the stage
  4. Sing that song you wrote about your high school experience to a crowd of people and make sure to aim your mouth towards the stool
  5. Enjoy!
